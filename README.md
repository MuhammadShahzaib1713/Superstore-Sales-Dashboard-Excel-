SUPERSTORE SALES DASHBOARD
Excel Project   |   Dataset: Sample Superstore (Kaggle)   |   Records: 9,994 Orders

ABOUT THIS PROJECT

I built this dashboard in Excel using the Sample Superstore dataset from Kaggle. The goal
was straightforward  take raw transactional sales data and turn it into something a sales
manager could actually read and act on without digging through rows of numbers.

The dataset covers orders from a fictional US retail store across three categories: 
Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology. It includes details like order dates, customer 
segments, shipping modes, states, and profit margins.

I handled the full process from raw data to finished dashboard inside Excel using Power 
Query for cleaning, Pivot Tables for aggregation, and charts with slicers for the 
interactive layer.

WHAT THE DASHBOARD COVERS

The dashboard is built to answer five things at a glance:

    How are overall sales, profit, and orders trending?
    Which product category and sub-category is performing best?
    Which customer segment is generating the most revenue?
    Which US states are the most and least profitable?
    How do different shipping modes affect order volume?

DASHBOARD VISUALS

    KPI Cards         Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Orders, Profit Margin
    Line Chart        Monthly sales trend over time
    Bar Chart         Sales and profit by product category and sub-category
    Donut Chart       Revenue split by customer segment
    Map Chart         Profit distribution across US states
    Bar Chart         Order volume by shipping mode
    Slicers           Filter by Category, Region, Segment, and Year

KEY FINDINGS

    Technology had the highest profit margin despite lower order volume than Office Supplies
    Tables and Bookcases in the Furniture category were consistently unprofitable
    The Consumer segment drove the majority of total revenue
    California, New York, and Washington were the top three states by profit
    Standard Class shipping accounted for the largest share of orders by far

    Download the repository
    Open Superstore_Sales_Dashboard.xlsx in Microsoft Excel

    Use the slicers to filter by category, region, year, or customer segment
